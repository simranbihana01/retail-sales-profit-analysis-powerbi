# 📊 Retail Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard
## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover insights into revenue, profitability, and the impact of discounting on business performance.
The goal is to provide actionable business insights that support data-driven decision-making.
---
## 🎯 Business Objectives
* Analyze sales and profit performance across categories and regions
* Identify loss-making products and segments
* Understand the impact of discounting on profitability
* Provide strategic recommendations to improve margins
---
## 📊 Key Metrics
* Total Sales: 2.3M
* Total Profit: 286K
* Total Quantity: 38K
* Profit Margin: 12.5%

## 🔍 Business Insights
* Technology drives highest revenue and profit, while Furniture generates losses
* Discounts above 20% significantly reduce profitability
* Profit is concentrated in a few products, creating dependency risk
* Sales peak in Q4 indicating strong seasonal demand
* High sales do not always translate into high profit
---
## 💡 Strategic Actions
* Control discounting for low-margin and loss-making products
* Reprice or optimize cost structure for Furniture category
* Focus on scaling high-performing Technology segment
* Diversify product portfolio to reduce dependency risk
